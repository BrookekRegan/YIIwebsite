Our Team

The Youth Intelligence Institute is led by a passionate and driven team of high school and middle school students committed to advancing research and knowledge across a wide range of fields. Our board members and directors bring dedication and leadership to every aspect of the organization — from managing research publications and community outreach to growing our platform and planning youth events. Together, they work to ensure YII remains a space where young minds can contribute meaningful work, collaborate across disciplines, and make a real impact in their communities.
