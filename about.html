About Youth Intelligence Institute

We are a student-led research organization focused on AI, ethics, STEM education, and community impact.

Our Mission

We bring together students across disciplines to research emerging technology, share findings, and build community projects with purpose.

Research

We publish work in healthcare, computer science, STEM education, law and ethics, and community STEM initiatives.

Community

Our projects connect research to local outreach, youth STEM learning, and hands-on demonstrations.

Collaboration

We support student research, interdisciplinary teamwork, and presentations that make technical ideas easier to understand.
