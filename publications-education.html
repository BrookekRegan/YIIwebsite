The Future of Human Expression Through Artificial Intelligence

Amiyah Rockingham - 2026

As part of the The Future of Human Expression Through Artificial Intelligence series, this article explores how artificial intelligence is transforming the ways people create, communicate, and share ideas across literature, art, and other creative fields. It examines both the opportunities and challenges of AI-assisted creativity, emphasizing the importance of preserving human originality, emotional depth, and ethical responsibility. Ultimately, the article argues that the future of human expression depends on using AI as a tool to enhance creativity rather than replace the unique perspectives that define human imagination.
