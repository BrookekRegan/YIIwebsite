Building Trust in Artificial Intelligence: The Role of Law and Ethics in Responsible Innovation

Amiyah Rockingham - 2026

Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed modern society by improving efficiency and innovation across industries, but its rapid advancement has also introduced significant ethical and legal challenges related to bias, privacy, transparency, and accountability. This paper examines the importance of legal regulations and ethical frameworks in promoting responsible AI development while balancing technological progress with the protection of individual rights and public trust. Ultimately, it argues that effective governance and responsible innovation are essential to ensuring artificial intelligence benefits society in a fair, transparent, and ethical manner.
